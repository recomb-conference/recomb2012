Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology

Thank you for participating in RECOMB 2012, the 16th Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology! And our special congratulations to all the awardees:

RECOMB 2012 Best Poster Award:

Matthew Edwards and David Gifford for the poster "High-resolution genetic mapping with pooled sequencing".

RECOMB 2012 Best Poster Award:

Thomas Bonfert, Gergely Csaba, Ralf Zimmer and Caroline C. Friedel for the poster "A context-based approach to identify the most likely mapping for RNA-seq experiments".

RECOMB 2012 Best Paper Award:

Patrick Holloway, Krister Swenson, David Ardell and Nadia El-Mabrouk for the contribution "Evolution of Genome Organization by Duplication and Loss: a Linear Programming approach".

RECOMB 2012 Test of Time Award:

Amir Ben-Dor, Laurakay Bruhn, Nir Friedman, Iftach Nachman, Michèl Schummer and Zohar Yakhini for the contribution "Tissue Classification with Gene Expression Profiles" which had been presented at RECOMB 2000 and published in full length in Journal of Computational Biology.

RECOMB 2012 Test of Time Award:

Nir Friedman, Michal Linial, Iftach Nachman and Dana Pe´er for the contribution "Using Bayesian Network to Analyse Expression Data" which had been presented at RECOMB 2000 and published in full length in Journal of Computational Biology.
