Travel Fellowships

NSF
Thanks to the generous support from the National Science Foundation, we have some funds to
support student travel for RECOMB 2012. These awards are available to students in US institutions and to students with US passport studying abroad. Click here for detailed information and instructions.

Thanks to the ISCB support, we will be able to award more travel fellowships.
